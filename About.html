About PLDT

PLDT - Transforming the Philippines into a Digital Nation

Spend quality time with your loved ones without leaving your home. Today, family-sized Internet connections are within your reach.

PLDT is the leading telecommunications and digital services provider in the Philippines. Through its principal business groups—fixed line, wireless, and digital services—PLDT offers a wide range of telecommunications and multimedia services across the Philippines’ most extensive fiber optic backbone and fixed line, cellular, and satellite networks.

PLDT is listed on the Philippine Stock Exchange (PSE: TEL) and its American Depositary Shares are listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE: PHI). PLDT has one of the largest market capitalizations among Philippine-listed companies.

OUR MISSION

PLDT's mission is to deliver meaningful experiences, promote digital inclusion, and empower Filipinos by providing innovative telecommunications and digital services.

OUR VISION

PLDT envisions being the preferred digital partner of every Filipino, empowering them to fulfill their limitless potential and enabling a digital nation.

OUR VALUES

  • Customer Centricity - We put our customers at the heart of everything we do.
  • Agility - We embrace change, adapt quickly, and strive for continuous improvement.
  • Integrity - We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Passion for Excellence - We aim for the highest standards and deliver results with excellence.
  • Collaboration - We work together as one team to achieve our goals.
